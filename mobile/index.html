How to play

To play, simply download the app, register and pick six brands.

Use your judgement, see which brands are trending, which are in the news or simply choose the ones you like best.

Then sit back and watch their performance, and yours, as the game unfolds. Points are awarded according to their constantly changing rank, which is based on the gain in their stock price.

If you top the scoreboard at the end of the contest, a cash prize is yours. Match the top six to hit the jackpot. It’s that easy!

There are also prizes if the friends you introduce win, so long as you both enter the same contest – if they win, you win too!

Contests take place every Wednesday starting at 9am.

Sharing

My Crowd – awarded to the top three players who refer the most friends.

Sharer Bonus – awarded every time a referred friends wins, so long as both sharer and referrer enter the same contest. The more friends invited, the higher the chances of winning.

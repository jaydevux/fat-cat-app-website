Download Fat Cat™

Here at Fat Cat Gaming we started out with a mission to create an exciting mobile game that is social, entertaining and above all REWARDING.

With gamers buying into games that only offer points, credits and more levels as rewards for hours of devoted play, the question remained, "What do gamers really want?"

As is usually the case, the answer was deceptively simple. Jackpots… Big jackpots!

So we found a way of giving it.

The result is…

Launched in India to rave reviews, Lucky 6™ is an easy-to-play, patent-pending game that enables players to showcase their knowledge and skill to win cash prizes, in a similar fashion to Fantasy Football, with the added benefit of prizes for people who share the app and make referrals.

To play, simply download the Fat Cat™ app on your iPhone or Android, pick six brands, either by using your skill and judgement or just by selecting the ones you like best, and sit back and watch their performance, and yours, as the game unfolds.

Lucky 6 is perfectly positioned to appeal to a broad demographic, arguably even broader than fantasy sports. It is not a trading, investing or portfolio management game, just a game of strategy that is fun to play. And now we’re bringing it to North America.

Points are awarded according to the brands’ rank, which based on the gain in its stock price. If you top the scoreboard at the end of the game, you win a cash prize. Match the top six brands to scoop the jackpot. It’s that easy!

We have two more exciting games in the pipeline that can be played on the Fat Cat™ app, My Lucky 6™ and Team Lucky 6™. With My Lucky 6™ you can create your own game or join a pre-set game and compete against your friends on a winner-takes-all basis. Team Lucky 6™is slightly different - you invite five friends, create a team of six and compete against other teams.
